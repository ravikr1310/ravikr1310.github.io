ABOUT CHEFLM

A connoisseur in the art of cooking, a perfectionist to the core, a strict disciplinarian, and a true leader, Chef Lalit Kumar – Chef LM for close friends, colleagues & associates – has played a key role in bringing the chef’s profession and the artists behind it recognition and rewards. He has played vital role in promoting Indian cuisine globally. A national tourism award winner himself, Chef LM was the master brain behind the most popular annual Chef Awards, the mega annual event of ICF into a brand over the years.

Young Lalit wanted to pursue some professional courses after completing his Commerce degree programme from Delhi University. There were hardly many vocational programmes available in the country forty years ago. It was from one of his friends, who was employed in the Oberoi’s , that Lalit came to know about the chef’s profession. He didn’t think twice before enrolling into Oberoi Centre of Learning & Development (OCLD) for 3-years Kitchen Management diploma programme. His family was not very happy with his choice, but didn’t object as well.
